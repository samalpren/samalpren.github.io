Application Questions

Why are you applying to Nashville Software School?

I found NSS through course report when I was first beginning my research on what bootcamp path I wanted to take. I had it at the top of my list with a few other schools. Some of the other programs and schools would pull rank above it when I was comparing tuitions, enrollment dates, etc, however I kept finding myself back on the NSS website. My brother (he’s a successful software engineer himself) put the bug in me to checkout bootcamps and has been helping me weigh pros and cons. He said something that really stuck with me: "wouldn’t it be nice to be able to ask the person on your right when you’re stuck on a problem, or be able to speak to your teacher?" I spent a lot of time looking at past cohorts and their personal websites, or listening to alumni podcasts and kept getting more and more intrigued by NSS. The curriculum is superb and it’s in my home city. Needless to say NSS is #1 on my list and my dream bootcamp, so I would really love the opportunity to go!
